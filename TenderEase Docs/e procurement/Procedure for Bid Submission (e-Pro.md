* Procedure for Bid Submission (e-Procurement Mode)



* Bids shall be submitted online only through the Government of Andhra Pradesh e-Procurement portal https://tender.apeprocurement.gov.in

. Offline bids shall not be accepted under any circumstances.



* Bidders shall mandatorily register on the e-Procurement platform prior to bid submission. For registration and online bid submission, bidders may contact the e-Procurement Help Desk.



* Bid submission shall be authenticated using a valid Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) of the bidder. Bids not authenticated by DSC shall not be accepted on the e-Procurement platform.



* The bidder shall ensure that the encryption certificate available in the DSC token is identical to the certificate uploaded in the bidder’s profile on the e-Procurement system. Any non-submission of bids due to mismatch of certificates shall be the sole responsibility of the bidder.



* The bidder shall submit Eligibility/Qualification details, Technical Bid and Financial Bid in the standard online formats available on the e-Procurement portal.



* The bidder shall upload scanned copies of all relevant certificates, documents, and proofs, including documents in support of eligibility, technical qualification, and other tender requirements.



* All uploaded documents shall be digitally signed by the bidder, owning full responsibility for the correctness and authenticity of the information furnished.



* Payment of Earnest Money Deposit (EMD) through the Electronic Payment Gateway on the e-Procurement platform is mandatory. EMD shall be paid online through Net Banking / NEFT / RTGS / Credit Card / Debit Card using the designated payment gateways.



* Applicable GST and bank charges on EMD and transaction payments shall be borne by the bidder.



* The bidder shall upload the Proof of online payment (Remittance) / Bank Guarantee towards EMD on the e-Procurement portal, which shall be treated as the primary requirement for considering the bid responsive.



* Bidders are advised to remit EMD at least T-1 or T-2 days prior to the bid submission closing date to avoid last-minute delays and bid submission failures.



* As per G.O.Ms.No.174, I\&CAD Dept., dated 01-09-2008, submission of original hard copies at the bid stage is dispensed with.



* The successful bidder shall submit the original hard copies of all uploaded documents, including original Proof of EMD payment / BG, to the Tender Inviting Authority before entering into the agreement.



* Submission and timely receipt of original documents shall be the sole responsibility of the successful bidder, and the department shall not be responsible for any delay or non-receipt.



* Technical evaluation shall be carried out solely based on the documents uploaded online in the e-Procurement system. Price bids shall be opened only for technically responsive bidders.



* In case the successful bidder fails to submit original documents within the stipulated time, or if any discrepancy is noticed between uploaded documents and original hard copies, the bidder shall be suspended from participating in e-Procurement tenders for a period of three (3) years, the user ID shall be deactivated, and legal action including criminal prosecution may be initiated as per rules.



* All participating bidders shall pay a non-refundable transaction fee to M/s APTS through the e-Procurement payment gateway, as prescribed by the Government of Andhra Pradesh. Applicable GST and bank charges shall be borne by the bidder.



* The Corpus Fund, as prescribed by Government Orders, shall be paid by the successful bidder before entering into the agreement / issue of purchase order. No corpus fund shall be collected for works, goods, or services with ECV up to ₹10 lakhs.



* Bidders shall download the tender document, carefully read all terms and conditions, and regularly monitor the e-Procurement portal for Addenda / Corrigenda issued from time to time.



* Upon successful completion of bid submission, the system shall generate a Bid Submission Acknowledgement with a unique bid number. Bids for which acknowledgement is not generated shall be treated as invalid.



* The Government of Andhra Pradesh shall not be responsible for incomplete or unsuccessful bid submissions by bidders.
